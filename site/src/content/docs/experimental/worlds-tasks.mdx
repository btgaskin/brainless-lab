These features extend [Core worlds, tasks, and populations](/core/worlds-tasks-populations/).
A calibrated task outcome remains an operational measure, not a validated capacity.

The Experimental `:shoal_forage` task composes a fixed population, two persistent resource
objects, three regulated variables, and an observer-relative sector sensor in `ObjectWorld`.
It deliberately declares no scalar `task_outcome`: study protocols must declare their own
need-satisfaction endpoint and keep grouped movement and perceptual topology as separate
secondary analyses.
