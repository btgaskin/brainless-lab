The situated adapter retains the existing torus, forage, signalling, colour-bank, contact,
and fixed-population mortality behavior while using ordinary bodies and the shared ensemble
lifecycle.

This readiness means the historical adapter is exercised by examples and tests. It is not a
confirmed ecological model or evidence for collective intelligence, social transmission,
or multi-agent criticality.
