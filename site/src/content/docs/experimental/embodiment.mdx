These features compose through the [Core embodiment contract](/core/embodiment/).
Integration does not imply biological or robotic fidelity.

`SectorVision` provides observer-relative, fixed-width banks for objects or conspecifics.
`AntagonisticTurnActuator` maps left-turn, right-turn, and thrust outputs to a unicycle command,
so reservoir activity can vary both turn and speed. `ProximityExposure` is a world relation:
it can restore a regulated association variable from physical proximity independently of
what an agent can see. All three are **Experimental** components used by
[the moving-shoal pilot](/experiments/shoal-vision-sweep/), not a calibrated fish model.
