Every measure here will carry its **equation**, a one-line interpretation, and a
**Source** callout to its Julia implementation. The suite spans three scales.

- **Node-level** — branching ratio (the subsampling-robust MR estimator), avalanche size/duration exponents + the crackling-noise relation, spectral radius $\rho(W)$, per-node distance-to-target.
- **Collective-level** — polarization, milling, cohesion, swarm regime, correlation length.
- **Cross-level** — the *same* estimators computed at the node scale and the agent scale (agent-scale activity = per-tick turn-event counts), so node-vs-agent covariation is a plot; plus susceptibility, Fano factor, participation ratio at both levels.
- **Information** — transfer entropy, within the node network and between agents.

:::note
A homeostatically rate-pinned $\sigma \approx 1$ is not by itself criticality — so
$\sigma$ and $\rho(W)$ are shown together.
:::
