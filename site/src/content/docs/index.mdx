## Two ways in

<CardGrid>
  <Card title="Cellular cognition" icon="seti:julia">
    Design and **evolve** node models under a strict locality constraint, then
    **benchmark** their self-organising competence across tasks.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Collective intelligence" icon="puzzle">
    Read the collective as a **network** and a **multi-agent system** at once —
    **profile** and **sweep** to map criticality, order, and information flow.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

:::note[Reservoir-computing *spirit*, not classical reservoir computing]
Recurrent spiking pools read out by simple effectors — but the weights **adapt online**
(Falandays) or are **evolved** (compartmental), with **no trained readout**. The name is
a nod, not a claim.
:::
